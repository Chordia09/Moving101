MOVING IN




Once you have found the appartment of your dreams you need to get ready to Move in. As an international student you probably would not have seen the appartment in person, so you need to be prepared for literally anything! Some key points to keep in mind to make the process easier




  • Register a move in date and time
  • Bring all required Documentation
  • Reserve any kind of move-in help being provided
  • Check everyhting in your space and make note of any damages
  • Put in repair requests if needed
